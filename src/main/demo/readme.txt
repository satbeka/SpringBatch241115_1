Copy 2 tables from DB1 to DB2. Create 2tables in DB2 and copy data from DB1.
For PostgreSQL DB. It have variant for Oracle DB also.

1.Copy .\demo to your work directory
2.Set your DB1 dataSource and DB2 dataSource2 in property file: 
 .\demo\src\main\resources\spring-config.xml
3.Set your DB1 table(t1) and DB1 table(t2) in property file: 
 .\demo\src\main\resources\job-read-files.xml
4.Run .\demo\cmd_081215.bat

Compiled .\demo\app081215.jar 091215
